Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Community, Care & Change

Ummeed Foundation is a non-profit organisation dedicated to empowering underprivileged children, protecting the environment and enabling sustainable livelihoods across rural and semi-urban India.

From classrooms to forests, our volunteers and partners work hand-in-hand with local communities to design programs that last — not just for a season, but for generations.

  • ✓ Community-first, locally led programs
  • ✓ 100% transparent fund utilisation
  • ✓ Partnerships with schools, hospitals & local bodies
Why We Exist

Our Mission & Vision

Two ideas guide every program we run — the change we work toward every day, and the world we're working to build.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide every child, family and community we work with access to education, healthcare, livelihood support and a clean environment — delivered with dignity and built to be self-sustaining long after we step back.

🌍

Our Vision

A future where no child is denied an education, no family goes without basic care, and every community has the tools, skills and green resources to thrive independently.

Our Programs

Six Projects, One Mission

Each of our flagship programs tackles a different root cause — together, they form a complete cycle of care.

01
Education

BachpanShala

Free, joyful learning centres for underprivileged children in urban slums and rural hamlets.

02
Healthcare

SEVA

Mobile health camps and medical aid bringing essential care to villages with no nearby clinic.

03
Animal Welfare

JEEV

Rescue, rehabilitation and vaccination drives protecting stray and farm animals across our regions.

04
Livelihood

UDAAN

Vocational training and micro-loans that help young adults and women launch small businesses.

05
Environment

PRAKRITI

Tree plantation, water conservation and clean-energy drives to restore local ecosystems.

06
Rural Development

VIKAS

Building sanitation, clean water access and basic infrastructure in the villages we serve.
